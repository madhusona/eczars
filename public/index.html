TelemarkhelperBOT

                  The solution we provide addresses the problem of mobility, time consumption and cost efficiency. Smart phones are very common now a days. Since Telegram being open source and allows creation of bots, we use telegram to provide the student details to the user. All that the user has to do is to send a request in the prescribed format to a telegram bot. The bot will immediately respond with the requested student detail. hence the solution over comes the burden of sending post cards, messages and logging in to Anna University website.
